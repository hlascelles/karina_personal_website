**Ingredients:**

* good tomatoes
* mozzarella
* basil
<nbsp></nbsp>
* salt
* olive oil
* balsamic vinegar


**Methods:**

* Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella. Serve with basil, sprinkled with salt and drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
