**Ingredients:**

* Chickpeas
* Cucumbers or cherry tomatoes (or both)
* Feta
* Olive oil
* Za'atar spice

**Methods:**

* Warm up the chickpeas (from frozen or from a can) in a microwave.
* Cut cucumbers/tomatoes and feta into bite size pieces.
* Mix everything, dress with olive oil and za'atar.
